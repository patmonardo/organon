# Alignment Charter: Philosophical and Statistical Reflection

Purpose: Keep all implementation work in Organon aligned with a single rationale.

## Core Thesis

Statistical Learning is necessary but not self-grounding. It operates within conceptual validity at the level of Understanding. Reason provides the rationale that grounds, limits, and orients this validity toward Truth as Oneness.

Short form:
- Spirit manifests truth as validity.
- Reason secures truth as Oneness.
- Knowledge is truth in determinate being.

## Foundational Map

Primary reflective dyad:
- <I am, I think>

Interpretation:
- These are co-arising, not one-way inference.
- The living event is reflexive awakening, prior in principle to space-time articulation.

Triadic movement used in this project:
- <sattva> -> <sat, sunya, bhava> -> <tamas>

Operational interpretation:
- Triadic Concept is Understanding in act.
- Truth becomes determinate as Knowledge through this movement.

## Philosophical Position for Engineering

1) Do not reject empiricism; legislate it.
- Empirical fit is required.
- Fit alone is insufficient as rationale.

2) Do not reject ESL; sublate it.
- Preserve optimization, generalization, and bias-variance discipline.
- Critique magnitude-only framing when it replaces rationale.

3) Keep architectonic priority explicit.
- Reason is prior in grounding.
- Understanding is prior in execution.
- Spirit is necessary for appearance and institutionalized validity.

4) Maintain the Fichte-Hegel bridge.
- Fichte supplies self-grounding origin (self-realization).
- Hegel supplies mediated development (objective testing and reconciliation).
- System design must carry both.

## System Mapping (Organon)

- GDS Kernel: empirical mediation and statistical learning operations.
- TS Agent: reflective orchestration, sequencing, intervention, reinterpretation.
- Agential Logic: rationale layer that explains why operations count as knowledge.

Non-negotiable relation:
- Kernel without rationale drifts to technique.
- Rationale without kernel drifts to abstraction.
- Agent binds the two in iterative mediation.

## Bias-Variance Reframing

Treat bias-variance as epistemic structure, not only a tuning knob.

- Bias: determinate one-sidedness needed for stability.
- Variance: instability of determination across realizations.
- Learning: mediated progression toward concrete adequacy.

## Assistant Alignment Rules

For code, docs, and design decisions, assistants must:

1) State the layer: Kernel, Agent, or Logic.
2) State the target: empirical adequacy, conceptual validity, or rationale coherence.
3) Preserve the bridge: no implementation that severs fit from rationale.
4) Avoid reductionism: do not collapse project intent into generic ML optimization.
5) Prefer explicit vocabulary over drift or synonym inflation.

## Acceptance Invariants

Every substantial change should preserve all three:

1) Empirical Adequacy Invariant
- Behavior is testable and data-facing claims are operational.

2) Reflexive Consistency Invariant
- Agent behavior is explainable in terms of project concepts and constraints.

3) Dialectical Mediation Invariant
- Contradictions are handled by integration and refinement, not suppression.

## Canonical Statement

Statistical Learning is the disciplined validity-work of Understanding; Reason is the rationale that grounds and unifies that validity; Spirit is the field in which this truth appears as shared and operative knowledge.
