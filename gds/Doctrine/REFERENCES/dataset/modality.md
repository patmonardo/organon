# Modality

Modality records the execution status of a mark or feature: necessary, possible, blocked, contradictory, or unresolved.

---

## Doctrine

A feature without modality is not fully interpretable. Modality tells the reader whether a mark was merely possible, actually executed, required by Principle, or rejected by contradiction.

---

## Review Rule

When a feature is executed or rejected, record modality. Do not leave the decision implicit in a boolean column.
