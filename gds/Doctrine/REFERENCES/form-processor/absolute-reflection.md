# Absolute Reflection

Absolute Reflection is Moment 7: the threshold where the six relative moments are gathered and presented for Principle evaluation.

---

## Doctrine

Absolute Reflection is not Concept. It is the prepared unity of reflection, standing before Principle.

Concept emerges only after Principle is satisfied.

---

## ZeroCopy Boundary

Absolute Reflection is the kernel-agent meeting point. The Rust kernel and agential processor must inspect the same shape data without marshaling. This is the architectural reason ZeroCopy matters.

---

## Review Rule

Do not describe Absolute Reflection as final knowledge. It is the threshold. Principle still has to decide.
