# PureForm Kernel Shape

PureForm is the kernel-side shape language that carries Form, Context, and Morph.

---

## Triad

- **Form**: the identity-bearing shape
- **Context**: the conditions and dependencies
- **Morph**: the operator or transformation chain

---

## Doctrine

PureForm belongs to the Rust kernel. It is the executable shape side of the system. The agential side reads it at the Absolute Reflection boundary to evaluate Principle.

---

## Review Rule

Do not make applications call kernel internals directly. PureForm is surfaced through procedures, Dataset artifacts, or service operations.
