ML Models Workbench fixtures

These fixtures are graph-free datasets for researching gds/src/ml/models directly.
They are intentionally below procedure/pipeline examples: DenseFeatures, labels,
targets, train sets, trainers, classifiers, and regressors are the object of study.

Files:
- logistic-separated.json: small binary classification fixture for logistic regression.
- linear-affine.json: small affine regression fixture for linear regression.
