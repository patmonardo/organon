- Processes moments as species within genus

### 4. Context Evaluator (`context-evaluator.ts`)
- **Context = Judgment**
- Processes presuppositions, scope, conditions

### 5. Morph Evaluator (`morph-evaluator.ts`)
- **Morph = Syllogism** (recursive structure)
- Can recursively invoke GDS Algos as Procedures
- Processes patterns as syllogistic premises/grounds
