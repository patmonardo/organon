### Chunk 3: Inverse ratio — qualitative determination
**ID**: `be-b-1-inverse-ratio`
**Source Lines**: 24-52
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "Consequently, the difference present in it is"
- Ends: "having to be in itself the whole."

**Analysis**: Difference = quantitative and external. Two different quanta of same substrate = their sum (quantum). Indifference = fixed measure, absolute limit, not quantum, not in opposition. Abstract determinateness falls into indifference. Two quanta alterable, restricted by fixed limit of sum, related negatively = qualitative determination. Stand in inverse ratio. Differs from formal inverted ratio: limit = real substrate, each side = whole.

**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

### Chunk 8: Inverse quantitative relation — externally determined
**ID**: `be-b-2-b-inverse-quantitative-relation`
**Source Lines**: 130-144
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "(b) The two moments are in inverse quantitative relation;"

**Analysis**: (b) Two moments = inverse quantitative relation, fluctuating on scale. Not determined by indifference (indifference of fluctuation), only externally. Determining appeal to other outside indifference. Absolute as indifference has second defect: determinateness not determined by absolute itself. ### Chunk 9: Quantitative determinateness as subsistence — contradiction
**ID**: `be-b-2-c-quantitative-determinateness-subsistence`
**Source Lines**: 146-169
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "(c) The quantitative determinateness of the moments which are now"
- Ends: "as we must now see."

**Analysis**: (c) Quantitative determinateness = mode of subsistence. Existence withdrawn from transitoriness of quality. But have subsistence of own (differs from quantitative existence) = indifference itself, each = unity of two qualities. Difference restricted: one quality more on one side, less on other (inverse). Each side = totality of indifference. Each quality = same sum, continues without restriction by quantitative limit. Determinations = immediate opposition, develops into contradiction.

### Chunk 10: Qualitative connection — equilibrium
**ID**: `be-b-3-qualitative-connection-equilibrium`
**Source Lines**: 171-195
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "Namely, each quality enters inside"
- Ends: "and would do so in the same proportion."

**Analysis**: Each quality enters each side in connection with other (supposed quantitative difference). If both self-subsistent, determinateness falls apart, unity/totality empty names. But determined as inseparable unity, meaning/reality only in qualitative connection. Quantitativeness = qualitative nature, each reaches only as far as other. If differ as quanta, one reaches beyond other (indifferent existence). As qualitatively connected, each only insofar as other is. Result = equilibrium: one increases/decreases, other same proportion.

### Chunk 11: Contradiction — essence
**ID**: `be-b-3-contradiction-essence`
**Source Lines**: 197-229
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "On the basis, therefore, of their qualitative connection,"
- Ends: "This is essence."

**Analysis**: On basis of qualitative connection: no quantitative difference, no more of one quality. More exceeding other = unstable determination or other itself. In equality, neither there (existence rests on inequality). Each factor vanishes (exceeds or equal). Vanishing = disturbance of equilibrium, one factor greater, other quality sublated, first predominant, other diminishes, becomes self-subsistent factor, no longer two moments but one whole. Unity as totality of determining process, determined as indifference = contradiction all around. Must be posited as self-sublating contradiction, determined as subsistence existing for itself, unity immanently negative and absolute = essence.
