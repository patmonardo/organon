### Chunk 2: Qualitative nature: reciprocal limiting
**ID**: `inverse-qualitative-nature`
**Source Lines**: 72-100
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:

**Analysis**: Qualitative nature: Quantum is self-determining, displaying limit within. (1) Immediate magnitude, affirmative quantum. (2) Immediate determinateness is limit - distinguished into two quanta. Quantum is unity of unit/amount (product). Exponent is affirmative moment and negation (unity). ### Chunk 3: Reciprocal limiting: each contains other
**ID**: `inverse-reciprocal-limiting`
**Source Lines**: 102-123
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "In accordance with these determinations,"
- Ends: "and therefore inseparable from it."

**Analysis**: Reciprocal limiting: Two moments limit themselves inside exponent. Each is negative of other. One becomes smaller as other becomes greater. Each possesses magnitude to extent it is magnitude other lacks. Each continues into other negatively. Each contains other, is proportioned to it - magnitude of other indispensable.

### Chunk 4: Bad infinity: sides approximate exponent
**ID**: `inverse-bad-infinity`
**Source Lines**: 125-192
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "This continuity of each in the other"
- Ends: "simply the exponent."

**Analysis**: Bad infinity: Unity (exponent) is in-itself of each. Each can take from other only to make equal to in-itself (maximum in exponent). Each loses determination making equal to in-itself - contradiction (in-itself vs moment). Exponent is limit - sides approximate but cannot attain (bad infinity). But infinite (exponent) is affirmatively present - simple quantum of exponent, unity of two.

### Chunk 5: Transition to ratio of powers
**ID**: `inverse-transition-powers`
**Source Lines**: 194-268
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "3. With this, however, we have"
- Ends: "The ratio is hereby determined as the ratio of powers."

**Analysis**: Transition to powers: Exponent's fixity develops as mediation of itself with itself in its other (finite moments). Negation of negation posited (affirmative self-relation). Exponent as product is unity of unit/amount - encloses moments, refers itself to itself. In inverse ratio, difference developed into externality. Qualitative being rejoins itself in externally existent otherness. Exponent preserves itself in negation of indifferent subsistence - determining factor in self-surpassing. Ratio determined as ratio of powers.
