**Text**: "1. The categorical syllogism has" through "this other premise is also categorical"

**Key Points**:

- More specific signification: middle term = objective universality

## Chunk 5: Hypothetical - Adds Immediacy; A as Middle Term (lines 204-226)

**Text**: "1. The hypothetical judgment contains only" through "This needs closer examination"

**Key Points**:
- Hypothetical judgment contains only necessary connection (without immediacy of connected terms)
- "If A is, so is B" (being of A = being of other B; not said A is or B is)
- Hypothetical syllogism adds immediacy of being: If A is, so is B, But A is, Therefore B is
- Minor premise expresses immediate being of A
- Not only this added: conclusion contains connection (not abstract copula but accomplished mediating unity)
- Being of A to be taken not as mere immediacy but essentially as middle term

**Keywords**: necessary connection, without immediacy, being of A, being of other, adds immediacy, immediate being, accomplished mediating unity, essentially as middle term
