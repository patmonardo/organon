## Overview

Being-for-itself is the completion of qualitative being—infinite being. In being-for-itself, the distinction between being and determinateness (negation) is posited and equalized. Quality, otherness, limit, reality, in-itselfness, ought are incomplete configurations of negation in being. But since in finitude negation has passed over into infinity, in the posited negation of negation, negation is simple self-reference and equalization with being: absolutely determinate being. Being-for-itself is the polemical, negative relating to the limiting other and, through this negation of the other, is being-reflected-within-itself.
