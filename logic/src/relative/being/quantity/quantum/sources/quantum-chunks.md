### Chunk 2: Number's determinateness: self-referring limit
**ID**: `quantum-number-determinateness`
**Source Lines**: 56-86
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:

**ID**: `quantum-intensive-magnitude-degree`

### Chunk 4: Degree's externality and quality
**ID**: `quantum-degree-externality-quality`

**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Analysis**: Degree's externality/quality: Self-reference is externality to itself. Degree has determinateness outside it (plurality external). Amount sublated in extensive is posited outside intensive. Degree is unitary determinateness among plurality of intensities, each in essential connection - scale is continuous progress. In externality it possesses its quality.

### Chunk 6: Identity: extensive and intensive are same determinateness
**ID**: `quantum-identity-two-sides`
**Source Lines**: 199-254
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:
- Starts: "The determinateness of the intensive magnitude is"
- Ends: "which, as negative, is the something posited as indifferent to them."

**Analysis**: Identity: Extensive/intensive are same determinateness. Distinguished only by amount within vs without. Extensive passes into intensive (many collapses into oneness). But intensive has determinateness in amount - essentially extensive. With this identity, qualitative something appears - unity through negation of distinct terms.

### Chunk 7: Alteration of quantum: quantum must alter
**ID**: `quantum-alteration-necessity`

**Analysis**: Alteration: Difference extensive/intensive is indifferent to quantum as quantum. Quantum is determinateness posited as sublated - indifferent limit that is negation of itself. Quantum is in absolute continuity with externality/otherness. It must alter - has being only in continuity with other. Limit becomes, not just exists.

### Chunk 8: Quantum sends itself beyond itself: to infinity
**ID**: `quantum-sends-beyond-infinity`
**Source Lines**: 283-304
**Status**: ✓ VERIFIED

**Text Boundaries**:

**Analysis**: Quantum sends beyond: Quantum is infinite (self-referring negation), repels itself from itself. Repulsion of determinateness from itself (not like itself but otherness). Quantum sends itself beyond itself (increases/decreases). Other it becomes is quantum impelling itself beyond - limit sublates and sends to further limit, to infinity.
