## Chunk Details

### Chunk 1: World raises itself to kingdom of laws
**Lines**: 4-23
**Title**: World raises itself to kingdom of laws
**Key Points**:
- Concrete existing world tranquilly raises itself to kingdom of laws
- Null content of manifold determinate being has subsistence in other
- Subsistence is therefore dissolution
- In other, that which appears comes to itself
- Appearance in changing also enduring, positedness is law
- Law is simple identity of appearance with itself
- Substrate and not ground, not negative unity
- Simple identity, immediate unity, abstract unity
- Other content also occurs alongside
- Content holds together internally, negative reflection inside itself
- Reflected into other, other is concrete existence of appearance
- Appearing things have grounds and conditions in other appearing things

### Chunk 2: Law — negative reflection
**Lines**: 25-47
**Title**: Law — negative reflection
**Key Points**:
- Law is also other of appearance as appearance
- Negative reflection as in its other
- Content of appearance differing from content of law
- Concrete existent which has negativity for ground
- Reflected into non-being
- Other is concrete existent likewise reflected into non-being
- Same, that which appears reflected not into other but into itself
- Reflection of positedness into itself is law
- As something that appears, essentially reflected into non-being
- Identity essentially just as much negativity and other
- Immanent reflection of appearance, law, not only identical substrate
- Appearance has in law its opposite, law is negative unity

### Chunk 3: Determination of law altered — negative unity
**Lines**: 49-77
**Title**: Determination of law altered — negative unity
**Key Points**:
- Determination of law altered within law itself
- At first, law only diversified content and formal reflection of positedness into itself
- Positedness of one side is positedness of other side
- But because also negative reflection into itself
- Sides behave not only as different but negatively referring to each other
- If law considered for itself, sides indifferent to each other
- But no less sublated through identity
- Positedness of one is positedness of other
- Subsistence of each also non-subsistence of itself
- Positedness of one side in other is negative unity
- Each positedness not only of that side but also of other
- Each side itself negative unity
- Positive identity in law as such at first only inner unity
- Stands in need of proof and mediation
- Negative unity not yet posited in them
- But since sides determined as different in negative unity
- Each contains other within while repelling otherness from itself
- Identity of law now posited and real

### Chunk 4: Law obtains negative moment — world-in-itself
**Lines**: 79-104
**Title**: Law obtains negative moment — world-in-itself
**Key Points**:
- Law has obtained missing moment of negative form of sides
- Moment previously belonged to appearance
- Concrete existence returned into itself fully
- Reflected itself into absolute otherness which has determinate being-in-and-for-itself
- That which was law no longer only one side of whole
- Essential totality of appearance
- Obtains moment of unessentiality that belonged to latter
- But as reflected unessentiality that has determinate being in itself
- Essential negativity
- As immediate content, law determined in general, distinguished from other laws
- Indeterminate multitude
- But because explicitly essential negativity
- No longer contains indifferent, accidental content determination
- Content rather every determinateness essentially connected together
- Totalizing connection
- Appearance reflected-into-itself is world that discloses itself above world of appearance
- One which is in and for itself

### Chunk 5: Kingdom of laws — suprasensible world
**Lines**: 106-155
**Title**: Kingdom of laws — suprasensible world
**Key Points**:
- Kingdom of laws contains simple, unchanging but diversified content
- Of concretely existing world
- But because total reflection of this world
- Also contains moment of essenceless manifoldness
- Moment of alterability and alteration, reflected into itself and essential
- Absolute negativity or form in general as such
- Moments have reality of self-subsisting but reflected concrete existence
- In world that has determinate being in-and-for-itself
- Reflected self-subsistence has form in it
- Content not mere manifold but content holding itself together essentially
- World in and for itself also called suprasensible world
- Concretely existing world characterized as sensible
- Intended for intuition, immediate attitude of consciousness
- Suprasensible world likewise has immediate, concrete existence
- But reflected, essential concrete existence
- Essence has no immediate existence yet
- But it is, in more profound sense than being
- Thing is beginning of reflected concrete existence
- Immediacy not yet posited, not yet essential or reflected
- But in truth not immediate which is simply there
- Things posited only as things of another, suprasensible, world
- First as true concrete existences
- Second as truth in contrast to that which just is
- Recognized: being distinguished from immediate being
- This being is true concrete existence
- Sense-representation ascribing concrete existence only to immediate being overcome
- Unconscious reflection overcome
- Determinations are reflected concrete existences

### Chunk 6: World-in-itself — totality
**Lines**: 157-181
**Title**: World-in-itself — totality
**Key Points**:
- World in and for itself is totality of concrete existence
- Outside it nothing
- Within it, absolute negativity or form
- Immanent reflection is negative self-reference
- Contains opposition, splits internally
- As world of senses and world of otherness or world of appearance
- Since totality, also only one side of totality
- Constitutes self-subsistence different from world of appearance
- World of appearance has negative unity in essential world
- To which it founders and into which returns as to ground
- Essential world is positing ground of world of appearances
- Contains absolute form essentially
- Sublates self-identity, makes itself into positedness
- As posited immediacy, is world of appearance

### Chunk 7: Determinate ground — opposition
**Lines**: 183-240
**Title**: Determinate ground — opposition
**Key Points**:
- Not only ground in general but determinate ground
- Already as kingdom of laws manifold of content
- Essential content of world of appearance
- As ground with content, determinate ground of other world
- But such only according to content
- World of appearance had other and manifold content than kingdom of laws
- Negative moment still peculiarly its own
- But because kingdom of laws now has this moment in it
- Totality of content of world of appearance
- Ground of all manifoldness
- But at same time negative of manifoldness
- World opposed to it
- In identity of two worlds, one essential, other posited and unessential
- Connection of ground restored
- Restored as ground-connection of appearance
- Not connection of two sides of identical content
- Nor mere diversified content like law
- But total connection, negative identity and essential connection
- Of opposed sides of content
- Kingdom of laws not only positedness of content is positedness of other
- But identity essentially also negative unity
- In negative unity each side is its other content
- Other not other in general but its other
- Equally containing content determination of other
- Two sides opposed
- Kingdom of laws has negative moment, opposition
- As totality, splits into world in-and-for-itself and world of appearance
- Identity of two is essential connection of opposition
- Connection of ground is opposition which in contradiction foundered to ground
- Concrete existence is ground that has come to itself
- Concrete existence becomes appearance
- Ground sublated in concrete existence
- Reinstates itself as return of appearance into itself
- As sublated ground, ground-connection of opposite determinations
- Identity essentially becoming and transition
- No longer connection of ground as such

### Chunk 8: World-in-itself — inversion
**Lines**: 242-258
**Title**: World-in-itself — inversion
**Key Points**:
- World in-and-for-itself itself world distinguished within itself
- In total compass of manifold content
- Identical with world of appearance or posited world
- To this extent its ground
- But identity connection at same time determined as opposition
- Form of world of appearance is reflection into its otherness
- World of appearance in world in-and-for-itself truly returned into itself
- In such manner that other world is its opposite
