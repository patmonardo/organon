Summary:

Key points: (KeyPoint)

- sourceFile: `src/logos/ys/ys_iv_29.ts`

- lineEnd: 53

Summary:

Key points: (KeyPoint)

- k3. _Dharma-megha_ does not mean a literal "cloud of virtue." It operates as a master algebraic discriminator (_prati-saṁkhyā-nirodha_), illuminating the exact boundaries of all phenomenal properties (_dharmas_).

### Entry ys-iv-033 — The Final Definition of Sequence (Absolute Necessity)

Span:

- sourceFile: `src/logos/ys/ys_iv_33.ts`
- lineStart: 3
- lineEnd: 31

Summary:

- k3. This is the shift from relative phenomenal time to the "Absolute Necessity" of the meta-law (the _dharma_ of _dharma-megha_). The logic of reality is finally comprehended outside of the flow of mutation.
