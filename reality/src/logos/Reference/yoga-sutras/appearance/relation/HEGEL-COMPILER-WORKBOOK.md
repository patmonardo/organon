Summary:

- sourceFile: `src/logos/ys/ys_iv_20.ts`

### Entry heg-iv-021 — Mediated Recognition of the Other

Span:

- sourceFile: `src/logos/ys/ys_iv_21.ts`

Summary:
The prohibition of mind-mind transparency tracks Hegel's phenomenology of Recognition. Subjective spirits cannot immediately intuit the inward depths of another spirit; all knowing of the "Other" is necessarily mediated through the external domain of Objective Spirit and labor.
