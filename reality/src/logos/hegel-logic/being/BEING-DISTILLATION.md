### BG1. Being Is the Beginning of Science

**Distillation:**
Being is the first sphere because logic must begin with what is immediate and least presupposed. But this beginning cannot remain a flat immediacy. Being immediately drives into its own differentiations and thereby reveals itself as only the first sphere of the concept.

**Structural Moves:**

- Being is the logical beginning.
- It begins as immediacy.
- It cannot remain merely immediate.

---

### BG2. Quality Is Being As Immediate Determinateness

**Distillation:**
Quality is the first explicit form of being because determinateness is still one with being. Being, nothing, becoming, existence, and being-for-itself are the opening arc of this immediacy.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quality is immediate determinateness.
- Determinateness is one with being.
- The section culminates in quantity.

---

### BG3. Quantity Is Being As Sublated Determinateness

**Distillation:**
Quantity is quality become negative and indifferent. Determinateness no longer clings immediately to being, but survives as magnitude, quantum, and ratio. This is being in the form of externality that is still held within itself.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantity is sublated quality.
- Determinateness becomes indifferent magnitude.
- Ratio prepares measure.

---

### BG4. Measure Is Qualitatively Determined Quantity

**Distillation:**
Measure reunites quality and quantity. It is the final and richest form of being, because externality is already taken back into self-reference. For that reason, measure is the point where being exhausts itself and passes into essence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Measure unites quality and quantity.
- It is the concrete truth of being.
- The section culminates in essence.

---

### BG5. The Sphere of Being Is Complete Only In Its Passage Beyond Itself

**Distillation:**
Taken as a whole, the Being folder is not a static ontology of first terms but the completed first movement of logic: immediate determinateness, indifferent determinateness, and their reunion. Its truth is therefore not still being, but essence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Being unfolds through three determinations.
- Each stage overcomes the one before it.
- The whole passes into essence.
