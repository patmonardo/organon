### BE1. Absolute Indifference Is Measure Reduced To Substrate

**Distillation:**
Absolute indifference is the final determination of being, but not yet essence. Measure has fallen back into a substrate where determinateness appears as state, external and vanishing.

**Structural Moves:**

- Indifference is measure reduced.
- Determinateness is only external state.
- The substrate remains one.

---

### BE2. Inverse Ratio Makes the Contradiction Explicit

**Distillation:**
Indifference does not remain calm self-sameness. Its factors stand in inverse ratio, each containing the whole and yet differing only quantitatively. This arrangement becomes contradictory and forces indifference beyond itself.

**Structural Moves:**

- The factors stand in inverse ratio.
- Each contains the whole.
- Quantitative difference becomes contradiction.

---

### BE3. Essence Is Being Returned Into Itself

**Distillation:**
The resolution is not a new being but the disappearance of immediacy as such. The presupposed totality becomes simple self-reference through the sublation of being. That result is essence.

**Structural Moves:**

- External reflection sublates itself.
- Determinations become mere moments.
- Being passes into essence.
