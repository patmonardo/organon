### MS1. Measure Is the Unity of Quality and Quantity

**Distillation:**
Measure begins where quality and quantity no longer stand over against one another. Externality now refers itself to itself, so the quantitative moment is already taken back into qualitative determination. Measure is therefore the concrete truth of being.

**Structural Moves:**

- Measure unites quality and quantity.
- Externality is referred back into itself.
- Measure is the concrete truth of being.

---

### MS2. Specific Quantity Gives Immediate Measure

**Distillation:**
The first field is specific quantity. A thing has a measure proper to it, and quantitative alteration is no longer indifferent to its quality. Measure first appears as specific quantum, then as specifying rule, and then as a relation of qualities within one measure.

**Structural Moves:**

- Every existence has a measure.
- Quantitative alteration reaches quality.
- Measure specifies external quantum.
- Measure becomes relation of qualities.

---

### MS3. Real Measure Gives Independent Measures, Nodal Lines, and the Measureless

**Distillation:**
In real measure, measures no longer belong merely to one thing inwardly but confront one another as independent measure-relations. Their combinations, elective affinities, nodal transitions, and collapse into the measureless show that measure cannot remain immediate.

**Structural Moves:**

- Measures become independent relations.
- Quantitative comparison becomes series and affinity.
- Nodal lines interrupt gradualness.
- Measure collapses into the measureless.

---

### MS4. The Becoming of Essence Is Measure's Own Truth

**Distillation:**
The measureless is not mere disorder but the point where measure reveals its own underlying substrate and contradiction. Absolute indifference and the inverse ratio of its factors drive the sphere beyond being and into essence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Measure returns to underlying indifference.
- Difference becomes only externally quantitative.
- Indifference becomes contradiction.
- Measure passes into essence.

---

### MS5. Measure Is the Closing Band of Being

**Distillation:**
Taken as a whole, the Measure folder is the final architectonic band of the Sphere of Being. It gathers quality and quantity into one existent unity, destabilizes that unity through real measure, and discloses essence as the truth of being.

**Structural Moves:**

- Measure receives the handoff from quantity.
- It completes the sphere of being.
- It opens the threshold of essence.
