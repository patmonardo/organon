### SQ1. Specific Quantity Makes Quantum Qualitative

**Distillation:**
Specific quantity is immediate measure. Here a thing's quantum is no longer indifferent to what it is. Alter the measure far enough, and the thing perishes or becomes another quality.

**Structural Moves:**

- Every existence has its measure.
- Quantum becomes a qualitative threshold.
- Mere increase and decrease are not enough.

---

### SQ2. Measure Specifies External Quantum

**Distillation:**
Measure is not only a fixed amount but an active specifying relation. It takes external quantitative alteration and assimilates it according to its own qualitative character. In this way, measure is already an immanent rule.

**Structural Moves:**

- Measure is a rule.
- It transforms external quantum.
- Its specificity lies in the exponent of relation.

---

### SQ3. Being-for-Itself in Measure Produces Real Measure

**Distillation:**
Once the two sides of measure stand as qualities whose relation is itself measure, the unity becomes a self-subsistent something. Measure is now ready to appear as a relation among independent measures.

**Structural Moves:**

- Qualities relate through one measure.
- Their unity becomes a self-subsistent whole.
- Specific quantity passes into real measure.
