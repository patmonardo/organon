### BG1. Pure Being Is Empty Immediacy

**Distillation:**
Pure being contains no further determination and no internal difference. It is therefore not rich origin but empty immediacy. In its truth, such purity cannot remain a distinct beginning.

**Structural Moves:**

- Pure being is indeterminate immediacy.
- It contains no inner difference.
- It cannot remain self-standing.

---

### BG2. Pure Nothing Is The Same Empty Immediacy

**Distillation:**
Nothing is not a different content set beside being. It is the same empty intuiting and thinking. The difference between being and nothing therefore vanishes as soon as each is taken in its purity.

**Structural Moves:**

- Nothing is pure emptiness.
- It is not a richer contrary to being.
- Being and nothing are the same in their purity.

---

### BG3. Becoming Is Their Truth

**Distillation:**
The truth is not that being and nothing are simply identical, nor that they are simply separate, but that each immediately vanishes in its opposite. This movement is becoming. In its quiescent result, becoming passes into existence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Being and nothing immediately vanish into one another.
- Becoming is their truth.
- Becoming settles into existence.
