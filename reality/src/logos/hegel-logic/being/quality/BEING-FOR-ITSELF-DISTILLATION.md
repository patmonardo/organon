### BF1. Being-for-Itself Is Self-Related Quality

**Distillation:**
Being-for-itself brings quality to completion because negation is no longer merely opposed within being but equalized with it. Quality now takes the form of self-reference.

**Structural Moves:**

- Negation is reflected into itself.
- Quality becomes self-related.
- The chapter opens with the one.

---

### BF2. The One Unfolds Into The Many

**Distillation:**
The one cannot remain inert singularity. In repelling itself, it becomes many ones. The many are not accidental additions but the own development of the one's self-reference.

**Structural Moves:**

- The one is immediate being-for-itself.
- Repulsion produces the many.
- The many belong to the one's own development.

---

### BF3. Repulsion and Attraction Carry Quality Into Quantity

**Distillation:**
Repulsion alone cannot stabilize plurality. Attraction is equally necessary, and their relation gives the full truth of being-for-itself. When their alternation reaches equilibrium, quality passes into quantity.

**Structural Moves:**

- Repulsion and attraction belong together.
- Their unity is the truth of plurality.
- The subchapter passes into quantity.
