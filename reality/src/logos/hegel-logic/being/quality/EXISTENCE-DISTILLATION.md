### EX1. Existence Is Determinate Being

**Distillation:**
Existence is determinate being, being whose determinateness is now existent quality. Something stands over against another, and quality appears as alterability, finitude, and inner negation.

**Structural Moves:**

- Existence is determinate being.
- Quality becomes existent determinateness.
- Something stands over against other.

---

### EX2. Finitude Is Not Final

**Distillation:**
The field of constitution, limit, and finitude shows that existence cannot remain closed within immediate determinate being. The finite points beyond itself and drives the whole chapter into infinity.

**Structural Moves:**

- Constitution and limit belong to existence.
- Finitude is internally unstable.
- The finite drives beyond itself.

---

### EX3. True Infinity Turns Into Being-for-Itself

**Distillation:**
The bad infinite alternates between finite and infinite as opposed terms. The true infinite is the affirmative unity that sublates this alternation. In that unity, existence turns into being-for-itself.

**Structural Moves:**

- The bad infinite alternates endlessly.
- The true infinite is affirmative unity.
- Existence passes into being-for-itself.
