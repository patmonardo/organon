### QL1. Quality Is Determinateness As Such

**Distillation:**
Quality is the first sphere of determinateness because being is no longer merely immediate but already implicated in what differentiates it. Indeterminateness itself proves to be a quality, so pure being cannot remain abstractly separate from determinateness.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quality is the first determinateness.
- Pure immediacy is already implicated in quality.
- Indeterminateness proves to be a qualitative determination.

---

### QL2. The `being/` Subchapter Gives the Vanishing Origin of Quality

**Distillation:**
In `being/`, pure being and pure nothing show themselves to be inseparable and vanish into becoming. The truth of immediate abstraction is therefore movement, and that movement settles into existence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Pure being is empty immediacy.
- Pure nothing is the same empty immediacy.
- Becoming is their truth.
- Becoming passes into existence.

---

### QL3. The `existence/` Subchapter Gives Quality As Finitude and Infinity

**Distillation:**
In `existence/`, determinateness becomes existent determinateness. Something, other, constitution, finitude, and infinity develop the field where quality now appears as real alterability and inner negation. The true infinite emerges only by overcoming the bad infinite's alternation.

**Structural Moves:**

- Determinateness becomes existence.
- Quality appears as finitude.
- The bad infinite is overcome.
- Existence turns toward being-for-itself.

---

### QL4. The `being-for-itself/` Subchapter Completes Quality

**Distillation:**
In `being-for-itself/`, qualitative negation becomes self-reference. The one, the many, repulsion, and attraction bring quality to its completed form. When repulsion and attraction reach equilibrium, quality passes over into quantity.

**Structural Moves:**

- Being-for-itself is negation reflected into itself.
- The one unfolds into the many.
- Repulsion and attraction mediate one another.
- Quality passes into quantity.

---

### QL5. Quality Is The Full First Arc of Being

**Distillation:**
Taken as a whole, the Quality folder moves from immediate abstraction, through determinate finitude, to self-related qualitative completion. It is therefore the first full architectonic band within the Sphere of Being.

**Structural Moves:**

- The first moment is pure immediacy.
- The middle moment is finite determinateness.
- The final moment is self-related quality.
- The whole band leads into quantity.
