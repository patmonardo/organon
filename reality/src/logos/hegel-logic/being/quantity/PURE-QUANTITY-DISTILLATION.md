### PQ1. Pure Quantity Is Sublated Being-for-Itself

**Distillation:**
Pure quantity is being-for-itself after the obduracy of the one has melted into continuity. It is self-same unity that continues through plurality without exclusion.

**Structural Moves:**

- Being-for-itself passes into attraction.
- Attraction is continuity.
- Quantity contains plurality without exclusion.

---

### PQ2. Continuity and Discreteness Are Both Moments of Quantity

**Distillation:**
Continuity and discreteness are not external alternatives. Each is the whole quantity in a one-sided emphasis. Continuous magnitude and discrete magnitude are therefore distinct only as moments of the same quantitative whole.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantity contains continuity and discreteness.
- Continuous magnitude is the whole quantity.
- Discrete magnitude is also the whole quantity.

---

### PQ3. Limiting Quantity Produces Quantum

**Distillation:**
When the many ones of discrete magnitude are gathered under a one that excludes, quantity becomes a determinate something, a quantum. The limit is no longer merely implicit but posited.

**Structural Moves:**

- The one becomes an enclosing limit.
- Quantity becomes determinate existence.
- Pure quantity passes into quantum.
