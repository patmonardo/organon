### QT1. Quantity Is Quality Become Indifferent To Being

**Distillation:**
Quantity begins where determinateness no longer holds itself as immediate qualitative exclusion. Determinateness is now indifferent to being. The limit is still present, but it is equally a no-limit, an externality within which being-for-itself continues into being-for-another.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantity is determinateness become indifferent.
- The limit is externalized.
- Being-for-itself continues in otherness.

---

### QT2. Pure Quantity Gives the Immediate Unity of Continuity and Discreteness

**Distillation:**
The first field is pure quantity. Here continuity and discreteness are not yet fixed as determinate quantum, but are taken as the underlying moments of magnitude itself. Quantity is still self-equal unity, though already constituted by plural ones.

**Structural Moves:**

- Pure quantity begins as continuity.
- Discreteness belongs within continuity.
- Limit turns pure quantity toward quantum.

---

### QT3. Quantum Gives Quantity As Determinate Limit

**Distillation:**
In quantum, quantitative determinateness is explicitly posited as limit. Number, extensive and intensive magnitude, and quantitative infinity develop the contradiction of a limit that is indifferent and yet determinative. Quantum is therefore unstable in itself and presses beyond itself.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantum is quantity with explicit limit.
- Number gives its first complete determinateness.
- Quantum becomes infinite progress.
- Infinity restores quality.

---

### QT4. Ratio Returns Quantity Into Qualitative Determination

**Distillation:**
Ratio is quantity that has its determinateness in another quantum. Direct ratio, inverse ratio, and the ratio of powers show quantity no longer merely transcending itself blindly but finding itself in its otherness. In this return, quantity acquires qualitative determination and passes into measure.

**Structural Moves:**

- Ratio gives quantum its determinateness in another quantum.
- The exponent becomes the qualitative center of relation.
- Quantity returns into itself in its externality.
- Ratio passes into measure.

---

### QT5. Quantity Is the Middle Band Between Quality and Measure

**Distillation:**
Taken as a whole, the Quantity folder is the full middle arc of the Sphere of Being. It begins from indifferent determinateness, develops quantitative limit and infinity, and culminates in relation as the threshold of measure.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantity receives the transition from quality.
- It develops indifferent limit through its contradictions.
- It completes itself in ratio.
- It hands off to measure.
