### QN1. Quantum Is Quantity With Explicit Limit

**Distillation:**
Quantum is quantity once limit is no longer merely implicit. Number gives this first complete determinateness, because unit and amount are now explicitly together.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantum is limited quantity.
- Number gives complete quantitative determinateness.
- Unit and amount are explicit moments.

---

### QN2. Extensive and Intensive Quantum Expose the Contradiction

**Distillation:**
Quantum first appears as extensive magnitude, then as intensive magnitude or degree. But the difference does not hold: intensive and extensive are each the same quantum under different placement of amount. The alterability of quantum is therefore essential.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantum becomes extensive and intensive.
- Degree gathers amount into simple determinateness.
- Extensive and intensive quantum are identical.
- Alteration belongs to quantum itself.

---

### QN3. Quantitative Infinity Restores Quality

**Distillation:**
In the infinite progress, quantum continually surpasses itself and yet remains quantum. The bad infinite proves inadequate. The truth is that quantum has its determinateness in this very externality, and thereby returns into qualitative determination as ratio.

**Structural Moves:**

- Quantum passes into infinite progress.
- Bad infinity is only repetition.
- The truth is the infinity of quantum.
- Quantum passes into ratio.
