### RA1. Ratio Is Quantum Determined In Its Other

**Distillation:**
Ratio begins when quantum no longer carries its determinateness as a merely indifferent limit. Instead, it is what it is in its relation to another quantum. Externality becomes the site of self-reference.

**Structural Moves:**

- Ratio gives determinateness in another quantum.
- Quantum finds itself in externality.
- The exponent centers the relation.

---

### RA2. Direct and Inverse Ratio Expose the Qualitative Moment

**Distillation:**
In direct ratio, the qualitative moment is still muted, carried by the exponent as common determination. In inverse ratio, the relation becomes more explicit: each side is what it is through the other's alteration, and the exponent comes forward as limit.

**Structural Moves:**

- Direct ratio gives immediate relation.
- Inverse ratio makes the exponent negative limit.
- The qualitative moment stands out.

---

### RA3. The Ratio of Powers Passes Into Measure

**Distillation:**
In the ratio of powers, quantum determines its own otherness. Quantity has now returned into itself in its externality and become quality again, not by abandoning quantity but by completing it. This truth of quantity is measure.

**Structural Moves:**

- Powers make quantum self-identical in its otherness.
- Quantity becomes quality in truth.
- Ratio passes into measure.
