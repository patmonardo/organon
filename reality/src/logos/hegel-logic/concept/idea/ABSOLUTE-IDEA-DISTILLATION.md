### AI1. Absolute Idea Reconciles Truth and Good

**Distillation:**
The Absolute Idea is the unity of the theoretical and practical Idea. What truth and good still held apart as striving and goal is now reconciled. The Idea no longer seeks itself in another, but rejoins itself in its own reality.

**Structural Moves:**

- Theoretical and practical Idea are reconciled.
- Striving is overcome in self-rejoining unity.
- The Idea is self-knowing truth.

---

### AI2. Method Is the Self-Movement of the Concept

**Distillation:**
The distinctive content of the Absolute Idea is method. Method is not an external tool of thinking but the concept's own self-determining movement. Its advance is analytic and synthetic at once, because the determinate comes from the subject matter itself.

**Structural Moves:**

- Method belongs to the concept itself.
- It is analytic and synthetic at once.
- Determination comes from within the subject matter.

---

### AI3. The Idea Freely Discharges Itself as Nature

**Distillation:**
The completion of the Absolute Idea is not a frozen conclusion. Because it is absolutely free, it determines itself as external idea. The transition to nature is thus a free release, not a collapse or loss.

**Structural Moves:**

- Completion remains active freedom.
- The Idea determines itself as external idea.
- The chapter passes into nature.
