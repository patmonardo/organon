### OJ1. Objectivity Is the Concept in Immediate External Existence

**Distillation:**
Objectivity is the concept that has given itself immediate external existence. The concept is no longer merely inwardly articulated as in subjectivity; it now stands in the form of a world of objects. Yet this externality is still the concept's own determination, not an alien region outside logic.

**Structural Moves:**

- The concept gives itself immediate existence.
- Externality is now explicit.
- This externality still belongs to the concept itself.

---

### OJ2. Mechanism Gives Objectivity in the Form of External Indifference

**Distillation:**
In `mechanism/`, objects stand outside one another in external self-subsistence. Their connections are real, but indifferent to what the objects are. The concept is present only as inner law or as merely external order, so objectivity appears first as a field of indifferent totalities.

**Structural Moves:**

- Objects are self-subsistent and external.
- Their unity is only inner law or outer arrangement.
- Objectivity begins as indifferent totality.

---

### OJ3. Chemism Gives Objectivity as Non-Indifferent Tension and Process

**Distillation:**
In `chemism/`, determinateness is no longer merely external. The object's reference to another belongs to its own nature, so objects stand in affinity, tension, and process. Objectivity now becomes a field of reciprocal incompleteness seeking real unity.

**Structural Moves:**

- Determinateness belongs to the object's nature.
- Objects stand in affinity and tension.
- Process seeks a real unity beyond external indifference.

---

### OJ4. Teleology Gives the Concept Back as the Truth of Objectivity

**Distillation:**
In `teleology/`, the unity that mechanism and chemism only implied becomes explicit as purpose. The concept now stands as self-determining, uses objectivity as its means, and realizes itself in it. Teleology is therefore the truth of the earlier objective forms.

**Structural Moves:**

- Purpose is the concept in objective existence.
- Mechanism and chemism are subordinated within it.
- Objectivity is taken up into self-determination.

---

### OJ5. Objectivity Culminates in the Idea

**Distillation:**
Teleology does not simply leave objectivity behind. It shows objectivity as already bearing the concept within it, so that realized purpose becomes inner purposiveness. At that point objectivity is no longer just the concept's externality but the beginning of its adequate truth, the Idea.

**Structural Moves:**

- Realized purpose is not mere external arrangement.
- Inner purposiveness is the truth of external purposiveness.
- Objectivity passes into the Idea.
