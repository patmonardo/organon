### TL1. Teleology Is the Concept in Free Concrete Existence

**Distillation:**
Teleology is the truth of mechanism and chemism because the concept now exists as purpose. The unity that earlier appeared only as inner law or restless process is explicitly posited as self-determining. Purpose is therefore the concept no longer lost in externality.

**Structural Moves:**

- Purpose is the concept in objective existence.
- Mechanism and chemism are preserved but subordinated.
- The concept is now explicitly self-determining.

---

### TL2. Subjective Purpose and the Means Still Presuppose Externality

**Distillation:**
At first purpose is still subjective and faces an external world as presupposition. It therefore needs a means. The means is the object penetrated by purpose, and the famous cunning of reason lies in letting that object bear the external labor while purpose preserves itself through it.

**Structural Moves:**

- Purpose first stands over against objectivity.
- It requires a means.
- The means is objectivity subordinated to purpose.
- The cunning of reason works through mediation.

---

### TL3. Realized Purpose Is the Truth of External Purposiveness

**Distillation:**
External purposiveness, taken by itself, yields only means and perishable products. Its truth appears only when the object is shown to be already inwardly fitted to purpose. Realized purpose therefore becomes inner purposiveness, where concept and objectivity are no longer merely externally connected. This is the threshold of the Idea.

**Structural Moves:**

- Mere external purpose remains inadequate.
- Realized purpose sublates the externality of the means.
- Inner purposiveness is the truth of teleology.
- Teleology passes into the Idea.
