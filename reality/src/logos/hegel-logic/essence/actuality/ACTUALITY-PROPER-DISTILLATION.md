### APR1. Actuality Is Manifestation

**Distillation:**
Actuality proper begins from the claim that the actual is manifestation. What is actual is not merely there but is the posited unity of reflection and immediacy. In its externality it is itself. That is why actuality stands higher than bare being or even concrete existence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Actuality is reflected absoluteness.
- The actual is manifestation.
- Externality is the actual's own self-showing.
- Actuality stands above being and concrete existence.

---

### APR2. Contingency Shows Formal Actuality, Possibility, and Necessity

**Distillation:**
The first movement is formal. The actual is immediately possible, the possible is immediately defective toward actuality, and their unity appears as contingency. Contingency is the restless conversion of actuality and possibility into one another. Yet this restlessness already contains necessity.

**Structural Moves:**

- The actual is formally possible.
- Possibility is only possibility and points beyond itself.
- Their immediate unity is contingency.
- Contingency already contains necessity.

---

### APR3. Relative Necessity Deepens Into Real Actuality

**Distillation:**
The second movement gathers content. Real actuality is not bare being but content preserving itself in its manifestation. Real possibility is the circle of conditions. When those conditions are complete, the fact is actually there. Necessity is now no longer empty conversion but a content-filled relation, though still relative because it begins from presupposed conditions.

**Structural Moves:**

- Real actuality carries content.
- Real possibility is the totality of conditions.
- Complete conditions yield the fact.
- Necessity is now real but still relative.

---

### APR4. Absolute Necessity Includes Contingency Within Itself

**Distillation:**
Absolute necessity is not necessity purified of contingency but necessity that has contingency within itself. It is being that is only because it is, essence that has itself for ground and condition. In its blind collapse of actualities, it reveals itself as the absolute's own exposition. Thus absolute necessity turns into substance.

**Structural Moves:**

- Absolute necessity is self-grounding.
- Contingency is internal to necessity.
- Blind collapse is the absolute's own exposition.
- Absolute necessity becomes substance.
