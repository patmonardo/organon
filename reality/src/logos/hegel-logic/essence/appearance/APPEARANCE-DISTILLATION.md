### AP1. Essence Must Appear

**Distillation:**
Appearance begins from the reversal of the first movement of Essence. Being had gone over into essence; now essence goes over into being, but into essential being, into concrete existence. The reflective shine that was still inward in the previous folder now completes itself in an element of relative self-subsistence. Appearance is therefore not a lesser illusion laid over essence from outside. It is essence in its own concrete existence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Essence makes itself into essential being.
- Reflection acquires an element of relative subsistence.
- Appearance is essence in concrete existence.
- The folder begins from the necessity that essence appear.

---

### AP2. The `thing/` Subfolder Gives the Immediate Appearance of Essence

**Distillation:**
The first appearance of essence is the thing. Here concrete existence still looks like an undifferentiated unity of essence and immediacy. Yet the thing cannot hold that simplicity. Its properties externalize it, its constitution out of matters disperses it, and its dissolution shows that the thing has its truth not in inert self-subsistence but in a mediated, porous, self-contradictory being. That is why the thing passes over into appearance proper.

**Structural Moves:**

- Concrete existence first appears as thing.
- Property and reciprocal action externalize thinghood.
- Matter reduces the thing to a merely collected `also`.
- Dissolution reveals appearance as the truth of the thing.

---

### AP3. The `world/` Subfolder Gives Appearance Its Lawful and Opposed Form

**Distillation:**
Once the thing has dissolved, appearance no longer means a single thing but a world. That world first stabilizes itself as law, the enduring self-identity of what appears. But law does not remain a quiet substrate. It develops into the opposition between the world of appearance and the world in itself. The chapter then shows that the two worlds are not independent realms but mutually inverting presentations of one totality.

**Structural Moves:**

- Appearance first stabilizes as law.
- Law becomes the substrate of the appearing world.
- A world in itself rises over against the world of appearance.
- Their opposition dissolves into a deeper unity.

---

### AP4. The `relation/` Subfolder Completes Appearance and Opens Actality

**Distillation:**
The truth of appearance is essential relation. This is the chapter in which the split worlds cease to stand apart as if their truth were elsewhere. Whole and parts show reciprocal conditioning collapsing into mediation. Force and expression deepen this into a self-preserving alteration. Outer and inner finally show that what something is, it is entirely in its externality. At that point appearance has exhausted itself and becomes actuality.

**Structural Moves:**

- Whole and parts expose mediated dependence.
- Force and expression make the relation immanent.
- Outer and inner collapse formal opposition.
- Appearance consummates itself in actuality.

---

### AP5. The Appearance Folder Now Has the Same Clean Layering As Reflection

**Distillation:**
With this structure, `appearance/` now has a true container layer at the root and chapter-level readable pairs below it. The folder-level artifacts stay architectonic, while `thing/`, `world/`, and `relation/` each receive a readable synthetic surface that can stand over the older detailed workbook stack. That preserves the middle scale that is working well in Essence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Container artifacts sit at the folder root.
- Chapter-level artifacts sit beneath them.
- The older local workbook stacks remain in place.
- The same pattern can now be carried into `actuality/`.
