### RL1. Essential Relation Is the Truth of Appearance

**Distillation:**
Essential relation is where appearance stops being the show of something elsewhere and becomes the structured unity of immediate and reflected self-subsistence. Appearance has not disappeared; it has been completed. Its truth is that each side is what it is only in its relation to the other.

**Structural Moves:**

- Relation is the truth of appearance.
- Immediate and reflected self-subsistence are united.
- Each side has its being only in its reference to the other.
- Appearance is completed rather than discarded.

---

### RL2. Whole and Parts Collapse External Self-Subsistence

**Distillation:**
Whole and parts is the first relational form. Each side seems self-subsistent, yet each has its being only in the other. The whole is nothing without parts, and the parts are nothing without the whole. This reciprocal conditioning destroys their supposed independence and yields mediation.

**Structural Moves:**

- Whole and parts first appear as reciprocal self-subsistences.
- Each presupposes the other.
- Their independence collapses into contradiction.
- The truth of the relation is mediation.

---

### RL3. Force and Expression Make the Relation Immanent

**Distillation:**
Force is the higher truth of whole and parts. The unity that was previously external now turns back into itself and expresses itself. Force passes into expression, expression returns into force, and the movement preserves itself through alteration. Relation is now dynamic and self-mediating.

**Structural Moves:**

- Force is the truth of whole and parts.
- Expression is force made external.
- The movement returns into itself.
- Relation becomes dynamic and immanent.

---

### RL4. Outer and Inner Show Complete Identity of Expression and Essence

**Distillation:**
Outer and inner completes the relational architecture. The inner is only inner because it is outer, and the outer is only outer because it is inner. What something is, it is wholly in its externality. Its appearance is the expression of its essence, and its essence consists in self-revelation.

**Structural Moves:**

- Inner and outer are immediately each the other.
- Form opposition becomes transparent.
- The outer expresses the inner fully.
- Essence is self-revealing.

---

### RL5. Appearance Consummates Itself In Actuality

**Distillation:**
Once outer and inner are fully identified, essential relation no longer remains within the appearance-band. The identity of essence and appearance has become actual. The chapter therefore stands as the true threshold to actuality.

**Structural Moves:**

- Outer and inner are completely mediated.
- Appearance and essence are identical in expression.
- Relation no longer remains merely relational.
- The chapter turns into actuality.
