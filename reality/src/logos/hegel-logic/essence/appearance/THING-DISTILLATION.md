### TG1. Concrete Existence First Appears As Thinghood

**Distillation:**
The first form of appearance is the thing. Concrete existence is no longer merely grounded but immediate through mediation; it is essence come forth into immediacy. In that form, it first presents itself as a thing, as a concrete existent whose negative unity is still held in immediate shape.

**Structural Moves:**

- Concrete existence is essence come forth into immediacy.
- This immediacy is mediated, not raw being.
- The first concrete existent is the thing.
- Thinghood is the opening appearance of essence.

---

### TG2. The Thing Externalizes Itself In Properties and Reciprocal Action

**Distillation:**
The thing first seems to have an inner self and an outer concrete existence. But that division cannot remain fixed. Property is not a dead attachment but the way the thing stands in relation to others. In reciprocal action the thing shows that its determinateness is not sealed within itself but distributed through external reference.

**Structural Moves:**

- Thing-in-itself and external concrete existence first divide.
- Property expresses the thing in externality.
- Reciprocal action reveals thinghood through relation.
- The thing cannot remain a simple inward unity.

---

### TG3. Property Passes Into Matter and the Thing Becomes a Mere Collection

**Distillation:**
Once the property's self-subsistence is stressed, it turns into matter or stuff. The thing is then no longer the true seat of determinateness but only the weak `this` in which multiple matters are externally gathered. The thing as this thing becomes nothing more than the quantitative `also` of indifferent materials.

**Structural Moves:**

- Property is treated as self-subsisting matter.
- Thinghood loses the power of inward unity.
- The `this` of the thing becomes merely external.
- The thing becomes a quantitative collection.

---

### TG4. Dissolution Reveals Porosity and Contradictory Mediation

**Distillation:**
The thing that is only a collection of free matters is absolutely alterable and dissoluble. Its materials pass in and out; its own being is porosity. Yet this is not just accidental breakdown. The thing shows itself as the contradictory mediation of self-subsistent matters through one another and through their negation. In that contradiction, thinghood consumes itself.

**Structural Moves:**

- The merely collected thing is absolutely alterable.
- Its bond is externally dissoluble.
- Matter is mutually porous and interpenetrating.
- The thing is self-contradictory mediation.

---

### TG5. The Truth of Thinghood Is Appearance

**Distillation:**
At the end of dissolution, concrete existence has reached its own truth: it subsists only in an other, indeed in its own nothingness as substrate. That is why the thing does not stand as the final form of appearance but only as its opening, and why the chapter passes into the world of appearance.

**Structural Moves:**

- The thing's truth is not isolated self-subsistence.
- Concrete existence subsists in an other.
- Thinghood founders into appearance.
- The chapter turns toward world-appearance.
