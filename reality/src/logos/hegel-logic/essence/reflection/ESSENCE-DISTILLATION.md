### ES1. Essence Begins As the Truth of Being, Not As a Second Thing Beside It

**Distillation:**
Reflection Within opens from a retrospective claim: essence issues from being. It is not immediate in and for itself, but the result of being's own movement of sublation. If essence is first taken as an immediate standing over against something else, that is only the first and weakest way it appears. The real point is already stronger: what stands over against essence is not an independent second term but only shine, and shine is essence's own positing.

**Structural Moves:**

- Essence issues from being as result, not as a separate substrate.
- Immediate opposition between essential and unessential is only a first appearance.
- What stands against essence is only shine.
- Shine is essence's own positing.

---

### ES2. The Essential/Unessential Split Collapses As an External Standpoint

**Distillation:**
The opening subsection first seems to distinguish the essential from the unessential. But Hegel quickly shows that this distinction still belongs to the sphere of existence and depends on an external taking-apart. The same content can be judged essential or unessential according to standpoint. So the first distinction does not yet give essence in its truth. It only shows the insufficiency of any merely comparative split.

**Structural Moves:**

- Essential and unessential first appear as a comparative partition.
- The distinction leaves the existence itself untouched.
- The same content can count as essential or unessential from different standpoints.
- The merely comparative split must therefore be overcome.

---

### ES3. Essence Is Absolute Negativity, and the Opposed Immediate Becomes Shine

**Distillation:**
Once the first comparison is pressed harder, essence is no longer just one side against another. It is absolute negativity of being. Being does not persist over against it as a self-subsistent unessential remainder. What remains immediate over against essence is null in and for itself. This remainder is not a thing, nor even a durable unessential. It is non-essence: shine.

**Structural Moves:**

- Essence is deepened from first negation to absolute negativity.
- Being does not persist except as what essence is.
- The opposed immediate is null in and for itself.
- The remainder is named non-essence or shine.

---

### ES4. Shine Is Non-Self-Subsistent Immediacy

**Distillation:**
Shine is what remains of the sphere of being once being has lost self-subsistence. Its being is only the sublatedness of being, being's nothingness in essence. It exists only in reference to another, only in negation. For that reason its immediacy is already reflective and mediated. Shine is immediate non-existence: an immediacy that has no weight of its own and is nothing apart from its relation to essence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Shine is the residue of sublated being.
- It exists only in negation and reference-to-another.
- Its immediacy is already reflected immediacy.
- Shine has no self-subsistence of its own.

---

### ES5. Shine Is Not the Shine of an Other but Essence's Own Shine

**Distillation:**
The chapter's key correction is methodological. The task is not to prove that shine somehow travels back into essence from outside, because being has already returned into essence in its totality. The real task is to show that the determinacies of shine are the determinacies of essence itself. Hence shine is not an alien appearance hanging on essence from outside. Shine is essence itself in the determinateness of being, but only as moment. Essence shines because it is internally self-differentiating negativity.

**Structural Moves:**

- The return of being into essence is already complete.
- The task is to show shine's determinacies as essence's own.
- Shine is essence in the determinateness of being.
- Determinateness here is only moment, not independent being.

---

### ES6. Reflection Is Becoming That Remains Within Itself

**Distillation:**
Reflection is shine no longer taken as immediate. It is the movement of becoming and transition that remains within itself. In the sphere of being, determinateness was grounded in an other. In reflection, the other is negation in itself, negation that has being only in self-reference. Reflection is therefore the movement from nothing to nothing and thereby back to itself. Its movement has no external substrate underneath it. The movement is the matter itself.

**Structural Moves:**

- Reflection is shine internalized.
- The other is now negation in itself, not an external other.
- Reflection moves from nothing to nothing and back to itself.
- There is no underlying substrate behind the movement.

---

### ES7. Positing Reflection Produces Immediacy As Positedness

**Distillation:**
The first explicit form of reflection is positing reflection. Here self-referring negativity coincides with itself and thereby produces immediacy, but only as self-sublating immediacy. This is positedness. Reflection does not begin from a ready-made immediate. The immediate first is only as the turning-back of negativity into itself. For that very reason positing reflection also becomes presupposing reflection: in sublating its own positing, it projects a beginning it then negates.

**Structural Moves:**

- Self-referring negativity coincides with itself as immediacy.
- This immediacy is positedness, not raw givenness.
- Reflection exists only in the turning-back itself.
- Positing becomes presupposing.

---

### ES8. External Reflection Finds What It Has Itself Posited

**Distillation:**
External reflection seems to begin from an immediate given standing before it. It presupposes a being and treats that being as its starting point. Yet the chapter shows that this exteriority cannot hold. Reflection itself determines the immediate as negative and then sublates that determination. The immediate that appears to stand before reflection is found to be what reflection itself has posited. So external reflection is not truly external. Its externality collapses into immanent reflection.

**Structural Moves:**

- External reflection seems to begin from a given immediate.
- Reflection determines that immediate as negative.
- The immediate proves to be reflection's own positing.
- Externality collapses into immanence.

---

### ES9. Determining Reflection Fixes Essential Determinations

**Distillation:**
Determining reflection is the unity of positing and external reflection. Here positedness is no longer just negation in general but negation grounded in immanent reflection. This is why the determinations of reflection differ from the transient qualities of being. They persist by virtue of reflection's self-equality in negatedness. In this way the determinacies of essence appear as free essentialities: fixed, self-related, and no longer merely passing into an other. Determining reflection is therefore the threshold of the doctrine of essentialities.

**Structural Moves:**

- Determining reflection unites positing and external reflection.
- Positedness is now grounded in immanent reflectedness.
- Reflection-determinations persist as essential rather than transient.
- Free essentialities begin to appear.

---

### ES10. Essence Turns Toward Foundation

**Distillation:**
The whole opening section is not a closed meditation on inwardity. Reflection determines itself, its determinations become essentialities, and then essence, as the reflection of its own immanent determining, turns into foundation. So the end of this `essence/` movement is already a passage. Essence does not merely hide behind being; it actively prepares its own further determination into grounded existence and appearance.

**Structural Moves:**

- Reflection first determines itself.
- Its determinations become essentialities.
- Essence turns into foundation.
- The `essence/` folder is an opening movement, not a final resting place.
