## Quick orientation

- First question: what is being fixed here?
  Answer: the opening movement in which essence emerges as the truth of being, appears first as opposed to shine, then shows shine as its own moment, and finally becomes reflection.
- Second question: what is the central operator of this subfolder?
  Answer: negativity returning into itself, so that immediacy becomes reflective rather than given.
- Third question: where does this subfolder lead?
  Answer: to foundation, then onward into concrete existence and appearance.
