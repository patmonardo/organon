### GS1. Ground Is Essence Positing Itself As What It Already Is In Itself

**Distillation:**
The Ground chapter opens by restating the result of contradiction in a new register. Essence determines itself as ground. Ground is not a foreign predicate attached to essence from outside. It is the true determination of essence after the determinations of reflection have foundered. Essence becomes ground by positing itself as what it is in itself: self-sublating negativity that is identical with itself in that very negation.

**Structural Moves:**

- Essence determines itself as ground.
- Ground is the foundered truth of the determinations of reflection.
- Ground is determination as sublated determination.
- Essence posits itself as what it already is in itself.

---

### GS2. Ground Is Real Mediation, Not Mere Reflective Shining

**Distillation:**
Hegel sharply distinguishes ground from the earlier movements of pure and determining reflection. Pure reflection is only the shining of one in another. Ground, by contrast, is real mediation. It is essence turning back into itself through its non-being and thereby positing restored immediacy. Ground therefore already contains an existent side. The chapter then lays out its own triad: absolute ground, determinate ground, and condition.

**Structural Moves:**

- Pure reflection is only pure reference.
- Ground is real mediation of essence with itself.
- Ground restores immediacy as self-identical negative.
- The chapter unfolds through absolute ground, determinate ground, and condition.

---

### GS3. Absolute Ground Is The Reciprocal Reference of Form and Essence

**Distillation:**
Absolute ground first appears as the twofold determination of ground and grounded. Ground is non-posited essence; the grounded is positedness as positedness. Their unity is not a bare identity but essence distinct from its own mediation. This immediately introduces form. Form is not something externally imposed on essence. Form is the completed whole of reflection and has essence in its own identity, just as essence has absolute form in its negative nature. Their difference therefore sublates itself into reciprocal reference.

**Structural Moves:**

- Ground and grounded are two moments of one essence.
- Their unity is essence distinct from mediation.
- Form arises from the self-subsistence of the reflected determinations.
- Form and essence stand in absolute reciprocal reference.

---

### GS4. Form and Matter Do Not Stand As Independent Principles

**Distillation:**
When form refers itself to its identity as to another, essence is determined as formless identity, that is, as matter. Matter is the simple identity of essence as the other of form. Yet matter is not a self-standing principle and form is not an independent shaping force imposed from outside. Each presupposes the other only because each already contains the other implicitly. Matter has form locked up within it; form returns to matter as its own substrate. The point is not dualism but the self-mediation of one essential process.

**Structural Moves:**

- Matter is essence as formless identity.
- Form presupposes matter because it presupposes its own identity.
- Matter presupposes form because it is only matter through sublated negativity.
- Form and matter are one movement of self-mediation.

---

### GS5. Determinate Ground Makes Grounding Real By Introducing Diverse Content

**Distillation:**
Formal ground is still only the same content doubled once as ground and again as grounded. It is sufficient only in a formal sense. Real or determinate ground begins when the content itself becomes diverse. Then grounding is no longer tautological. Ground and grounded are different in content, and the question of ground genuinely asks for another content determination. But this realization also breaks the ground-connection into externality. Essential content and unessential manifold are tied together only by an external link.

**Structural Moves:**

- Formal ground doubles one content into two forms.
- Its sufficiency is only formal.
- Real ground introduces diverse content.
- This realization externalizes the ground-connection.

---

### GS6. Complete Ground Tries To Recover Unity From That Externality

**Distillation:**
Because real ground has externalized itself, it returns to a new ground. Complete ground gathers formal and real ground together. It reasserts identity of ground and grounded while preserving the diverse content that real ground introduced. This is not yet the final resolution, but it is the point where the ground-connection can no longer remain merely external. It is driven toward conditioning mediation.

**Structural Moves:**

- Real ground returns to another ground.
- Complete ground gathers formal and real ground together.
- Identity reasserts itself within diverse content.
- Ground passes toward conditioning mediation.

---

### GS7. Condition Exposes The Relative Unconditioned As Contradictory

**Distillation:**
Condition is the immediate manifold existence presupposed by ground. Yet this immediacy is not simply independent. It is already posited and indifferent to being condition, while ground is likewise only self-sufficient by presupposing what stands outside it. So condition and ground are each relatively unconditioned and yet each is also only a moment. Their relation is therefore contradictory: each appears independent while proving essentially mediated.

**Structural Moves:**

- Condition is immediate manifold existence.
- Ground presupposes condition.
- Condition is indifferent to being condition.
- Ground and condition are contradictory relative unconditioneds.

---

### GS8. The Absolutely Unconditioned Is The One Fact Of Ground and Condition Together

**Distillation:**
The contradiction of the relatively unconditioned resolves into the absolutely unconditioned. Ground and condition are shown to be one whole of form and content. Their reciprocal presupposition reduces to reflective shine. What truly underlies them is one substrate: the fact in itself. This absolute fact is both condition and ground, and its movement is to condition itself and place itself as ground over against its own conditions.

**Structural Moves:**

- Ground and condition first shine in one another.
- Their reciprocity resolves into one substrate.
- The truly unconditioned is the fact in itself.
- The fact conditions itself and grounds itself.

---

### GS9. Ground Ends By Passing Into Concrete Existence

**Distillation:**
The Ground chapter does not end in an inert fact behind appearances. The absolutely unconditioned is absolute ground only by positing conditions as its own presupposed immediacy. In that self-positing, mediation no longer stands over against what is mediated. The fact proceeds into concrete existence. Ground therefore completes the turn from reflective essence toward existent actuality.

**Structural Moves:**

- The absolute fact is absolute ground.
- It posits conditions as its own immediacy.
- Mediation rejoins itself in the fact.
- Ground passes into concrete existence.
