### RF1. Reflection Is a Folder-Level Container, Not Only the Name of the Opening Section

**Distillation:**
At the top level, `reflection/` should now be read as a container artifact field rather than only as the title of the opening section. Its internal movement begins with the `essence/` subfolder, where essence first shines within itself; it continues through `foundation/`, where the determinations of reflection become explicit as identity, difference, and contradiction; and it culminates in `ground/`, where those reflected determinations return into real mediation and pass toward concrete existence.

**Structural Moves:**

- `reflection/` is the high-level container.
- `essence/` gives the opening self-reflection movement.
- `foundation/` gives the doctrine of essentialities.
- `ground/` gives the mediation that passes toward existence.

---

### RF2. The `essence/` Subfolder Captures Reflection Within Proper

**Distillation:**
The `essence/` subfolder is where the opening section properly lives. Essence issues from being, faces shine, internalizes that shine, and becomes reflection. This is the inward opening of the folder as a whole. It is where immediacy first loses its naive independence and becomes reflective moment.

**Structural Moves:**

- Essence issues from being.
- Shine is essence's own positing.
- Reflection internalizes shine.
- The opening movement turns toward foundation.

---

### RF3. The `foundation/` Subfolder Fixes the Essentialities of Reflection

**Distillation:**
Once reflection is no longer merely the opening inward movement, its determinations must be fixed. That is the work of `foundation/`. Identity, difference, and contradiction are not three unrelated topics but the explicit doctrine of the essentialities that reflection already contained implicitly. Contradiction then shows itself as the engine that returns the movement into ground.

**Structural Moves:**

- Reflection becomes determined reflection or essentiality.
- Identity is the first essentiality.
- Difference and contradiction deepen the movement.
- Contradiction returns into ground.

---

### RF4. The `ground/` Subfolder Converts Reflection into Real Mediation

**Distillation:**
In `ground/`, reflection ceases to be only reflective shining and becomes real mediation. The chapter moves through absolute ground, determinate ground, and condition, until the fact in itself emerges as the unity of ground and condition. The folder therefore ends not in a static ground behind things but in the procession of the fact into concrete existence.

**Structural Moves:**

- Ground is real mediation.
- Absolute ground unfolds form, matter, and content.
- Determinate ground and condition externalize and regather the movement.
- The fact passes into concrete existence.

---

### RF5. The Reflection Folder Now Has the Right Top Layer

**Distillation:**
With this structure, the top layer of `reflection/` no longer duplicates the `essence/` subfolder. Instead it acts as the chapter-cluster container for the whole reflective band of Essence. That gives a clean folder-to-artifact pattern: the folder gets a container pair, and each major child folder gets its own readable pair beneath it. This is the pattern that should continue in `appearance/` and `actuality/` as well.

**Structural Moves:**

- Container artifacts sit at the folder root.
- Chapter-level artifacts sit one level below for major subfolders.
- The top layer stays architectonic rather than overly granular.
- The same pattern can govern the rest of Essence.
