## Arc

- Subjectivity opens the formal side of discursive cognition.
- Concept, judgment, and syllogism articulate the inner subjectivity triad.
- Life turns logical perfection into methodical construction and exposition.
- Cognition regulates scope, validity, articulation, and commitment.
- Idea gathers life and cognition into an architectonic bridge that remains on the General Logic side.
