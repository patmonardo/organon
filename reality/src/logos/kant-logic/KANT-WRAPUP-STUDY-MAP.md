# Kant Logic Wrap-Up Study Map

Role: derivative wrap-up map for the Kant logic artifact family
Authority: navigation and study guidance only

## Why this file exists

This note is for the wind-down pass.

The point is not to create one more doctrine-file, but to give a compact way to study the whole Kant artifact family without losing the specific gain of the recent Subjectivity and Logogenesis work. It should let the reading close on the Kantian side cleanly, while also keeping sight of why this material will later matter for the `@organon/logic` package and for graph, ML, and algorithm work.

## Main orientation

If the whole folder is too large to hold at once, use this order of importance:

1. Subjectivity as the formal container.
2. Logogenesis as the cross-family spine from judged object to syllogism.
3. General Logic as the inner machinery of discursive cognition.
4. Transcendental Logic as the object-validating and boundary-setting completion.
5. Framing and boundary notes as the wrap-up edge of the Kant pass.

The key practical decision is this: for the final Kant stretch, do not try to read everything flatly. Read everything by returning to one question: how does the object get taken into thought, made explicit in judgment, and completed in lawful mediation without breaking Kant's boundary?

## What to focus on now

### Subjectivity is the center of gravity

If there is one place to stand while reviewing the whole folder, it is `SUBJECTIVITY-WORKBOOK.md` together with `LOGOGENESIS-WORKBOOK.md`.

That combination gives the best current answer to the question: what is the formal machine of Kantian discursive cognition once the object-side and the judgment-side are read together?

Keep these points fixed:

- Subjectivity here is not psychology. It is the formal architectonic of discursive cognition.
- Concept, judgment, and syllogism are not separate topics laid side by side. They are internal regions of one container.
- The LG pass strengthens this by showing how the judged object is first front-loaded under quantity, quality, relation, and modality before it is carried through Subjectivity proper.
- The Object() boundary must remain Kantian: phenomenon and noumenon are not a license for positive supersensible science.

## The trio question

For the current wrap-up, Kant should be allowed to stay Kantian even when the pressure toward the larger trio remains visible.

Use the trio only as a set of orientation checks:

- Against Leibniz: Kant's boundary work matters because pure understanding cannot directly legislate things in themselves for us.
- Against Hegel: the pressure toward one logic is real, but this folder should not prematurely fuse Kant's field into Objective Logic.
- Within Kant himself: Jasche General Logic and the CPR should be read as one split field rather than as strangers.

That is enough for the wrap-up. Anything stronger belongs in a separate comparative surface.

## Why this matters later for `@organon/logic`

The later beneficiary is not a literal import of Kantian doctrine into code. The benefit is structural.

- Subjectivity gives a model of formal container logic.
- Concept gives universality, marks, and ordered containment.
- Judgment gives explicit relation and admissible form.
- Syllogism gives lawful derivation and mediation.
- Life and Cognition give method, validity, and criteria of disciplined use.
- The fourfold object plus Object() boundary give a sharper sense of what objecthood, relation, and lawful restriction would mean before any graph procedure is built.

So the present work does not yet become graph ML or algorithms. It clarifies the form of the machine that later graph and algorithm work may instantiate more concretely.
