# Translation Decisions

1. Keep literal and technical layers separate.
2. Keep interpretation only in interpretive_note.
3. Use numeric topic naming: 1.1, 1.2, and so on.
4. Special paragraphs are not verses.
5. Section 1 special paragraph id: SKR.BS.1.1.PRE (Preamble).
6. Section 2 special paragraph id: SKR.BS.1.2.INT (Introduction).
7. Regular verses keep numeric ids, for example SKR.BS.1.1.001 for sutra 1.1.1.
