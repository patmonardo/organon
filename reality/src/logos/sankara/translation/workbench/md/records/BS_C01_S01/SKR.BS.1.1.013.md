brahma jijñāsitavyamityuktam | kiṃlakṣaṇakaṃ punastadbrahme tyata āha bhagavānsūtrakāraḥ —

## Translation

### Literal
It has been said that Brahman is to be inquired into. But what is the defining mark of that Brahman? Therefore the revered sutrakara says -

### Technical
Transition introducing the lakshana-sutra for Brahman.

### Interpretive Note
Sets up formal definition after programmatic call to inquiry.
