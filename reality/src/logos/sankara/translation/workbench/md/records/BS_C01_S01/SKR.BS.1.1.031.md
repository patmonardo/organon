### Source Text
एवं तावद्वेदान्तवाक्यानां ब्रह्मात्मावगतिप्रयोजनानां ब्रह्मात्मनि तात्पर्येण समन्वितानामन्तरेणापि कार्यानुप्रवेशं ब्रह्मणि पर्यवसानमुक्तम् । ब्रह्म च सर्वज्ञं सर्वशक्ति जगदुत्पत्तिस्थितिनाशकारणमित्युक्तम् । साङ्ख्यादयस्तु परिनिष्ठितं वस्तु प्रमाणान्तरगम्यमेवेति मन्यमानाः प्रधानादीनि कारणान्तराण्यनुमिमानास्तत्परतयैव वेदान्तवाक्यानि योजयन्ति । सर्वे ष्वे व वेदान्तवाक्येषु सृष्टिविषयेष्वनुमानेनैव कार्येण कारणं लिलक्षयिषितम् । प्रधान पुरुषसंयोगा नित्यानुमेया इति साङ्ख्या मन्यन्ते । काणादा स्त्वे तेभ्य एव वाक्येभ्य ईश्वरं निमित्तकारणमनुमिमते , अणूंश्च समवायिकारणम् । एवमन्येऽपि तार्किका वाक्याभासयुक्त्याभासावष्टम्भाः पूर्वपक्षवादिन इहोत्तिष्ठन्ते । तत्र पदवाक्यप्रमाणज्ञेनाचार्येण वेदान्तवाक्यानां ब्रह्मात्मावगतिपरत्वप्रदर्शनाय वाक्याभासयुक्त्याभासप्रतिपत्तयः पूर्वपक्षीकृत्य निराक्रियन्ते ॥

## Translation

### Literal
Thus far it has been shown that Vedanta sentences aimed at realization of Brahman-self culminate in Brahman even without insertion into action-framework; and Brahman has been stated as omniscient, omnipotent cause of world's origination, maintenance, and dissolution. Sankhya and others, however, holding that an established reality must be known by other pramanas, infer alternative causes such as Pradhana and then force Vedanta sentences into that direction. In all Vedanta creation-passages they seek to indicate cause from effect by inference alone. Sankhyas infer eternal conjunction of Pradhana and Purusha; Vaisesikas infer from those same passages Ishvara as efficient cause and atoms as material cause; other logicians too, relying on pseudo-textual and pseudo-reasoning supports, arise here as purvapaksha positions. Therefore, by the teacher skilled in word-sentence-pramana, these seeming textual and logical supports are first presented as opposing views and then refuted, in order to establish that Vedanta sentences are for realization of Brahman-self.

### Technical
Introductory framing for subsequent refutations: competing inferential cosmologies are staged as purvapaksha so Vedantic samanvaya toward Brahman-atman realization can be defended.

### Interpretive Note
Meta-hermeneutic transition from foundational method to structured polemic against rival causal systems.
