## Translation

### Literal
And because no statement of rejectability. (8)

### Technical
Sutra 1.1.8: sat cannot be Pradhana, since scripture never marks it as a provisional rejectable referent.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra compression of the heya-vacana argument.
