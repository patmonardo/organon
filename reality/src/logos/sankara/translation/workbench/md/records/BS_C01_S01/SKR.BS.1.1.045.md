## Translation

### Literal
Because of merger in sleep. (9)

### Technical
Sutra 1.1.9: sat is not Pradhana, since sleep-dissolution teaching implies a conscious ground.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra shorthand for svapya-linga argument.
