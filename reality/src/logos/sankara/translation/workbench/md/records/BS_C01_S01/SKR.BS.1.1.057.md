## Translation

### Literal
If it be said 'No, because it is a modification-word,' no - because of abundance. (13)

### Technical
Sutra 1.1.13: mayat in anandamaya is taken as pracurya, not vikara.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra settles the suffix dispute in favor of Brahman-reference.
