आनन्दमयाधिकारे च ‘ सोऽकामयत बहु स्यां प्रजायेयेति ’ (तै. उ. २ । ६ । १) इति कामयितृत्वनिर्देशात् नानुमानिकमपि साङ्ख्यपरिकल्पितमचेतनं प्रधानमानन्दमयत्वेन कारणत्वेन वा अपेक्षितव्यम् । ‘ ईक्षते र्नाशब्दम् ’ (ब्र. सू. १ । १ । ५) इति निराकृतमपि प्रधानं पूर्वसूत्रोदाहृतां कामयितृत्वश्रुतिमाश्रित्य प्रसङ्गात्पुनर्निराक्रियते गतिसामान्यप्रपञ्चनाय ॥ १८ ॥

## Translation

### Literal
And in the anandamaya section too, because there is mention of willing ('He desired: may I become many, may I be born'), one should not rely even on inferentially imagined Sankhya non-conscious Pradhana as anandamaya or as cause. Though Pradhana was already rejected by 'because of seeing, not from word,' it is rejected again here on account of the willing-text cited in the previous sutra, to further elaborate the common-drift argument. (18)

### Technical
Bhashya on 1.1.18 re-applies anti-Pradhana reasoning in anandamaya context: kama/intentional willing marks conscious cause and blocks anumana-based Pradhana import.

### Interpretive Note
Rejection is reiterated to secure consistency across all relevant passages.
