## Translation

### Literal
And because of desire; therefore no dependence on inference. (18)

### Technical
Sutra 1.1.18: kama-shruti excludes need for inferential Pradhana hypothesis.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra ties intentional willing directly to conscious Brahman-causality.
