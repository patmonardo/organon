## Translation

### Literal
If it be said 'No, because instruction differs,' no - since there is no contradiction in both. (27)

### Technical
Sutra 1.1.27: upadesha-bheda does not defeat identification, as both expression-forms are compatible with one referent.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra closes the locative/ablative objection.
