### Source Text
न वक्तुरात्मोपदेशादिति चेदध्यात्मसम्बन्धभूमा ह्यस्मिन् ॥ २९ ॥

### Transliteration (IAST)
na vakturātmopadeśāditi cedadhyātmasambandhabhūmā hyasmin || 29 ||

## Translation

### Literal
If it be said 'No, because the speaker teaches his own self,' no - because in this (chapter) there is predominance of adhyatma relation. (29)

### Technical
Sutra 1.1.29: vaktr-atmopadesha does not force deity reading; adhyatma-sambandha-bhuma supports Brahman.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra resolves narrative first-person ambiguity.
