## Translation

### Literal
If it be said 'No, due to jiva and main-prana indicators,' no - because of threefold upasana, because they are dependent (on Brahman), and because here they are connected with That. (31)

### Technical
Sutra 1.1.31: jiva/mukhya-prana lingas do not negate Brahman-reference, as they function as dependent upadhi-modes within tri-fold Brahman-upasana.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra closes the Kausitaki reconciliation by integrating all indicators under one referent.
