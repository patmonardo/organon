## Translation

### Literal
And smriti too shows difference between embodied self and Supreme Self, in passages such as: 'The Lord dwells in the heart-region of all beings, O Arjuna, causing all beings to revolve, mounted on machine-like structures, by maya.'

### Technical
Bhashya support line: smriti corroborates jiva-Ishvara distinction relevant to the adhikarana conclusion.

### Interpretive Note
Cross-text reinforcement from Gita-style antaryami language.
