अर्भकौकस्त्वात्तद्व्यपदेशाच्च नेति चेन्न निचाय्यत्वादेवं व्योमवच्च ॥ ७ ॥

arbhakaukastvāttadvyapadeśācca neti cenna nicāyyatvādevaṃ vyomavacca || 7 ||

## Translation

### Literal
If one says 'not so, because of small abode and such designation,' no; because this is due to focused contemplation - like space. (7)

### Technical
Sutra 1.2.7: arbhakaukas/minuteness language does not force jiva-reference; it is upasana-conditioned, analogous to localized speech about all-pervasive space.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra answer to the locality-size objection.
