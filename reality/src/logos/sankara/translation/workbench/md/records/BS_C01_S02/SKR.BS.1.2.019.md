### Transliteration (IAST)
sambhogaprāptiriti cenna vaiśeṣyāt || 8 ||

## Translation

### Literal
If one says enjoyment would result, no - because of distinction. (8)

### Technical
Sutra 1.2.8: bhoga does not accrue to Brahman despite all-pervasiveness/non-duality claims, due to decisive jiva-Ishvara distinction at relevant level.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra compression of the bhoga-transfer refutation.
