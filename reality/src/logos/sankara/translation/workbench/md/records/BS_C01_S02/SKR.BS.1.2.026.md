## Translation

### Literal
What is prima facie obtained is: intellect and knower-self. Why? Because of the qualifier 'entered the cave.' Whether cave means body or heart, intellect and knower-self can coherently be said to have entered there. And where that is possible, one should not force a spatially particular location upon all-pervasive Brahman. The phrase 'in the world of merit' also indicates not crossing karma-domain; but the Supreme does not move within merit/demerit range ('neither increased by good nor diminished by bad'). Also 'shadow and light' fits a conscious-inert pair, due to mutual contrast. Therefore one may take the pair here as intellect and knower-self. To that we reply -

### Technical
Purvapaksha refinement in 1.2.11: guhapravesha, sukrita-loka limitation, and chaya-tapa contrast are marshaled for buddhi-jiva pairing.

### Interpretive Note
Raises the strongest internal-semantic case against paramatma as one of the two.
