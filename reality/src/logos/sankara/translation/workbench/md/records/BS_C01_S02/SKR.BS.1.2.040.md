## Translation

### Literal
And for this reason too, the person in the eye is the Supreme Lord: the devayana path known in shruti as belonging to the knower of Brahman and one who has received the hidden teaching, and also known in smriti, is seen here as being taught for the knower of the eye-person. Beginning with mention of the archis path and continuing through sun, moon, lightning, and the non-human guide to Brahman, the text says those who proceed by this divine path do not return to the human cycle. Hence, by this well-known path associated with knowers of Brahman, the Brahmanhood of the eye-located person is decisively established. (16)
