## Translation

### Literal
And not the Smrti-based one, because attributes not belonging to it are spoken. (19)

### Technical
Sutra 1.2.19: Smrti-Pradhana is excluded as antaryamin since the passage attributes non-Pradhana properties.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra compression of the ataddharma criterion.
