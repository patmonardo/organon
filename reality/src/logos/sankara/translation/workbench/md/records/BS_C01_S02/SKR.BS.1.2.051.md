### Source Text
शारीरश्चोभयेऽपि हि भेदेनैनमधीयते ॥ २० ॥

### Transliteration (IAST)
śārīraścobhaye'pi hi bhedenainamadhīyate || 20 ||

## Translation

### Literal
And the embodied self too; for in both recensions this one is studied as distinct. (20)

### Technical
Sutra 1.2.20: jiva is not antaryamin, since both textual recensions treat it as distinct from the inner controller.

### Interpretive Note
Recensional evidence is used as a decisive exclusion marker.
