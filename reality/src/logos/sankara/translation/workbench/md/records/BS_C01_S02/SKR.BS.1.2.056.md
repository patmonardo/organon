## Translation

### Literal
And not the other two, because of qualifying attributes and declarations of distinction. (22)

### Technical
Sutra 1.2.22: bhuta-yoni is not jiva or Pradhana, established by visesana and bheda-vyapadesha indicators.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra condensation of the twofold exclusion test.
