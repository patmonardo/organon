### Source Text

### Transliteration (IAST)
smaryamāṇamanumānaṃ syāditi || 25 ||

## Translation

### Literal
That which is remembered may be an inferential indicator - thus. (25)

### Technical
Sutra 1.2.25: remembered (smrti) material can function as anumana-linga supporting Brahman-reference of Vaisvanara.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra compression of smrti-to-sruti inferential support.
