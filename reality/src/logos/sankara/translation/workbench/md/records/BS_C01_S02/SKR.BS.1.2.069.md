## Translation

### Literal
As for what was said - that because mantras show elemental fire connected with heaven and so on, limb-assignments like 'heaven is head' may belong to that fire, or to its presiding deity through lordly power - this must be refuted. It is said: for just the previously stated reasons, deity is not Vaishvanara; and elemental fire too is not Vaishvanara. For elemental fire, being only heat-and-light, cannot coherently be assigned heaven-head and similar total-organic structures; one modification cannot be inner-self of other modifications. Likewise for the deity: even if lordly capacities are granted, such heaven-head construction is not coherent, because it is not the causal ground and its lordship is dependent on the Supreme. And impossibility of the self-term for all these options remains established.

### Technical
Bhashya on 1.2.27 excludes bhutagni and devata candidates: neither supports causal-totality predicates nor atman-designation required by the passage.

### Interpretive Note
Final elimination of fire/deity alternatives by causal and semantic non-fit.
