### Source Text
सा च अम्बरान्तधृतिः परमेश्वरस्यैव कर्म । कस्मात् ? प्रशासनात् । प्रशासनं हीह श्रूयते — ‘ एतस्य वा अक्षरस्य प्रशासने गार्गि सूर्याचन्द्रमसौ विधृतौ तिष्ठतः ’ (बृ. उ. ३ । ८ । ९) इत्यादि । प्रशासनं च पारमेश्वरं कर्म । न अचेतनस्य प्रशासनं सम्भवति । न ह्यचेतनानां घटादिकारणानां मृदादीनां घटादिविषयं प्रशासनमस्ति ॥ ११ ॥

## Translation

### Literal
And that sustaining up to space is the action of the supreme Lord alone. Why? Because of governance. For scripture here teaches governance: 'Under the command of this Imperishable, O Gargi, sun and moon are held in place,' and so on. Governance is an act belonging to the Lord. Governance cannot belong to what is insentient. We never see insentient causes like clay, though causes of pots and the like, exercising governance over effects.

### Technical
Sutra 1.3.11 bhashya adds prasasana-laksana: cosmic order under command implies conscious lordship, excluding inert pradhana-like candidates.

### Interpretive Note
Moves from ontic support to active governance as Brahman-mark.
