## Translation

### Literal
One may object: if sustaining up to space just means an effect depends on its cause, then even for those who hold Pradhana as cause this could fit. How then is Brahman-hood inferred merely from sustaining up to space? To this, the next answer is read.

### Technical
Purvapaksha transition: causal-dependence alone is claimed insufficiently discriminative, preparing stricter exclusion criteria.
