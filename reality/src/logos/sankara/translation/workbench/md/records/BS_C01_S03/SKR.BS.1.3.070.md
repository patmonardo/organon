### Source Text
अथापि नाम प्रत्युच्चारणमन्येऽन्ये वर्णाः स्युः , तथापि प्रत्यभिज्ञालम्बनभावेन वर्णसामान्यानामवश्याभ्युपगन्तव्यत्वात् , या वर्णेष्वर्थप्रतिपादनप्रक्रिया रचिता सा सामान्येषु सञ्चारयितव्या । ततश्च नित्येभ्यः शब्देभ्यो देवादिव्यक्तीनां प्रभव इत्यविरुद्धम् ॥ २८ ॥

### Transliteration (IAST)
athāpi nāma pratyuccāraṇamanye'nye varṇāḥ syuḥ , tathāpi pratyabhijñālambanabhāvena varṇasāmānyānāmavaśyābhyupagantavyatvāt , yā varṇeṣvarthapratipādanaprakriyā racitā sā sāmānyeṣu sañcārayitavyā | tataśca nityebhyaḥ śabdebhyo devādivyaktīnāṃ prabhava ityaviruddham || 28 ||

## Translation

### Literal
Even if one grants that each re-utterance involves different phoneme-individuals, one must still admit phoneme-universals as the basis of recognition. Then the meaning-conveyance procedure established for phonemes must be transferred to those universals. Therefore there is no contradiction in saying that divine individuals arise from eternal words. (28)

### Technical
Concluding compromise: whether varna-individuals are transient or recurrent, semantic function anchors in enduring varna-samanya; thus shabda-nityatva remains intact.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra-tail consolidates both linguistic models into the same nityatva outcome.
