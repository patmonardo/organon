## Translation

### Literal
And because of sameness of names and forms, even in recurrence there is no contradiction - as seen in revelation and in smriti. (30)

### Technical
Sutra 1.3.30: cyclic recreation does not break Vedic authority when nama-rupa recur in stable pattern, supported by shruti-smriti evidence.

### Interpretive Note
Sutra condensation of recurrence-with-identity thesis.
