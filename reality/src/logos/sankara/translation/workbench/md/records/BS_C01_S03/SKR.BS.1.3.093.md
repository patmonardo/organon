### Transliteration (IAST)
itaśca na śūdrasyādhikāraḥ ; yatsatyavacanena śūdratvābhāve nirdhārite jābālaṃ gautama upanetumanuśāsituṃ ca pravavṛte — ‘ naitadabrāhmaṇo vivaktumarhati samidhaṃ somyāharopa tvā neṣye na satyādagāḥ ’ (chā. u. 4 | 4 | 5) iti śrutiliṅgāt || 37 ||

## Translation

### Literal
And for this reason too there is no eligibility for the Shudra: when truthfulness established absence of Shudra status, Gautama proceeded to initiate and instruct Jabala. This is indicated by the shruti marker: 'one who is not a brahmana could not speak thus; bring fuel, dear one, I shall initiate you - you have not departed from truth.' (37)

### Technical
Bhashya on 1.3.37 treats the Satyakama Jabala episode as linga: pedagogical initiation follows determination of non-Shudra status.
