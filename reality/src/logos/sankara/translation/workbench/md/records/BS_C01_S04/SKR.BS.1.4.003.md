## Translation

### Literal
If it be said that for some people the inferential entity is scripturally taught, no: because what is taken up is arranged in the body-metaphor, and this is also shown. (1)

### Technical
Sutra 1.4.1 denies that Katha's avyakta validates Sankhya Pradhana; the referent is fixed by ratha-rupaka contextual uptake.
