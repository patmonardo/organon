### Literal
Because there is no distinguishing specification, as in the case of the cup. (8)

### Technical
Sutra 1.4.8: the mantra does not uniquely fix Sankhya pradhana, since its descriptors are underdetermined, just as in the 'camasa' case where expression alone does not identify a specific referent.

Sutra-level rule: indeterminate imagery cannot ground exclusive doctrinal identification.
