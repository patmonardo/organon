## Translation

### Literal
There, in the case of the statement 'this is that head; this cup has its opening below and base above,' the sentence-completion provides determination of a specific cup. Here, however, concerning this aja, which one is to be determined? To that we now reply.

### Technical
Transition note: unlike the camasa example where vakhya-sesa disambiguates reference, the aja-mantra remains open unless constrained by broader context; the ensuing argument supplies that constraint.
