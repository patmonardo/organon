## Translation

### Literal
And because it is taught by figurative construction, there is no contradiction, as in the case of 'honey' and the like. (10)

### Technical
Sutra 1.4.10: non-literal pedagogical designation (kalpana-upadesa) is acceptable in sruti, just as in other known figurative teachings; therefore aja-language here does not force Sankhya literalism.

### Interpretive Note
