## Translation

### Literal
Instruction about deliberate willing also indicates that the Self is both agent and material basis: statements like 'May I become many, may I be born.' Deliberative initiative reveals agency; and since the same reality becomes the plurality, material causality is also implied.

### Technical
Abhidhya-upadesa supports dual causal status: volitional autonomy marks nimitta-kartṛtva, while self-manifestation into multiplicity indicates upadana identity.

### Interpretive Note
Volition language is treated as causal evidence, not mere poetic narrative.
