### Entry san-i-050-051 — Nirbīja Samādhi and Absolute Cessation

Span:

- sourceFile: `ys_i_50_51.ts`
- reference: Yoga Sūtra I.50-51

Summary:
Sūtras I.50 and I.51 document the catastrophic collapse of the Doctrine of Being and the transition into Essence. Patañjali declares: _taj-jaḥ saṃskāro 'nya-saṃskāra-pratibandhī_ (I.50) and _tasyāpi nirodhe sarva-nirodhān nirbījaḥ samādhiḥ_ (I.51). ("The latent impression/momentum [saṃskāra] born of that [truth-bearing insight] is an inhibitor/blocker [pratibandhī] of all other impressions. Upon the cessation [nirodhe] of even that, since all is ceased, there is seedless absorption [nirbījaḥ samādhiḥ].") The truth-bearing insight of Real Measure (I.48-49) is so absolute that it acts as a universal inhibitor, violently blocking all other finite, quantitative mental activity. But Real Measure itself is still a structural state. To achieve ultimate liberation (_nirbīja_, without seed), the inhibitor itself must playfully consume itself. When the structure of Measure is entirely negated ("even that" ceases), consciousness falls out of the immediate realm of Being entirely. It enters the deep, unmediated ground of Essence.

Key points: (KeyPoint)

- k1. **Pratibandhī (The Inhibitor):** The truth-bearing insight is structurally weaponized. It acts as an absolute block against the return of lower, discursive thoughts or ratios.
- k2. **Tasyāpi Nirodhe (The Cessation of Even That):** The ultimate dialectical twist. The very mechanism that saved the mind (Real Measure) must itself be destroyed/sublated. The inhibitor must inhibit itself.
- k3. **Sarva-Nirodhān (The Cessation of All):** The absolute collapse of the logical category of 'Being'. There are no more immediate objects, limits, or measures left to contemplate.
- k4. **Nirbījaḥ Samādhiḥ (Seedless Absorption):** The state completely free of structural dependency. It marks the total passage out of the immediate Doctrine of Being into the reflected depths of Essence.
