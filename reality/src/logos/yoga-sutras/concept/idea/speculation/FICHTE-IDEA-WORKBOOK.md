# Concept/Idea/Speculation - FICHTE IDEA WORKBOOK

## The Principle (_Bhasya_): The Absolute Solipsism of the Pure Act

If Life (YS III.16) internalizes time, and Cognition (YS III.17-20) internalizes meaning and other minds, then Speculation (YS III.21-22) is the ultimate capstone: the absolute and final internalization of empirical space. In Fichtean terms, this is the moment the "I" proves that it is not an object among objects.

**1. The Ego as an Object (The Body)**
In the empirical world, the ego constantly runs into a contradiction: it knows it is absolute, infinite, and free, yet it finds itself trapped in a finite physical body (_kāya-rūpa_) that is constantly being observed, bumped into, and acted upon by the Non-I. The ego feels like a vulnerable object.

Fichte's _Science of Knowing_ demands that this contradiction be resolved practically. If the physical body is merely the necessary spatial limitation posited by the "I" so that the "I" can act, then the "I" holds the absolute deed to the body.

**2. Suspending the Posited Rule**
Patañjali describes exactly how this philosophical realization translates into supernatural power. By applying _Samyama_ to the physical form (_kāya-rūpa_), the Yogin grasps the precise rule that makes the body visible (the _śakti_ of being grasped by the eye). Because the Yogin intellectually posited this rule in the first place, the Yogin can suspend it (_stambhe_).

The Yogin unplugs the connection (_asamprayoge_) between their posited body and the posited light of the physical world. The result is invisibility (_antardhānam_).

**3. The Triumph of Pure Subjectivity**
Invisibility is the ultimate Fichtean victory. By disappearing from the visual field, the Yogin proves that the Absolute "I" is not dependent on empirical manifestation. The Non-I (the observer) is completely defeated; it cannot find the Subject because the Subject has withdrawn into its pure, unconditioned Idea. The Yogin continues to exist in absolute certainty and power, but leaves no trace in the mechanical world. The ego has become pure act, impenetrable to objective physics. This resolves the final tension of Subjective Logic.
