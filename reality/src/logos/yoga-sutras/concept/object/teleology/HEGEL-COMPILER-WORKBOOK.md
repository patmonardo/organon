# YS III.15: Hegel Mapping - Teleology

## 1. Hegel's Doctrine of the Concept: Objectivity -> Teleology

In Hegel's _Science of Logic_, Objective Logic proceeds from the indifferent external laws of Mechanism, through the inner binding affinities of Chemism, to its ultimate truth: **Teleology**, or self-determining Purpose. Teleology reveals that the objective world is not a blind mechanical sequence, but is structured by and subordinated to the Concept acting as Purpose.

**YS III.15:** "The variation in the sequence (_krama_) is the cause (_hetu_) for the variation in the transformation (_pariṇāma_)."

### 1.1 The Subordination of Mechanism and Chemism

Hegel writes: "Purpose has resulted as the third to mechanism and chemism; it is their truth... mechanical causality, to which chemism is also in general to be added, still makes its appearance in this purposive connection which is the external one, but as subordinated to it and as sublated in and for itself."

Patañjali describes exactly this subordination. The _pariṇāma_ (the transformations mapped out in YS III.13-14 as mechanism and chemism) are not self-caused. They have a _hetu_ (a cause). This cause is the _krama_ (the sequence). The sequence is the purposive ordering of reality. The concept of Purpose in Hegel "returns to itself" out of the blind progression of cause and effect by taking control of the sequence of causes.

### 1.2 Purpose Altering the Sequence

Hegel views Teleology as the Concept posited _as within it referring to objectivity_. The subjective purpose uses the mechanical world as a "means." The mechanical object is "indifferent to its being determined." Because it is indifferent, a higher Purpose can step in, alter the sequence of determinations, and force a new outcome.

This is the exact definition of _kramānyatvaṃ_ (variation in the sequence). The Yogin's localized Concept (acting via _Samyama_) steps into the objective world, manipulates the sequence of physical states, and actualizes its subjective purpose as objective reality. The mind proves that it is the ultimate _hetu_ (cause) overriding pure physical determinism.
